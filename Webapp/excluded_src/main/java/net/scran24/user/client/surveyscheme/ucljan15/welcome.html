Welcome to the Teenage and Young Adult Health and Lifestyle Questionnaire!

This is a survey about health and lifestyle among young people who have been diagnosed with cancer.

The study is being carried out by researchers at University College London in collaboration with CLIC Sargent.

There are two parts to the survey:

Part 1 will ask about your food intake over the past 24 hours. Please try to answer as honestly as possible. This section will take around 15-20 minutes to complete.

Part 2 asks about your health and your lifestyle. We will also ask questions about the information you received about lifestyle when you were diagnosed with cancer and what kind of information you would have liked to receive. This section will take around 15-30 minutes to complete.

By completing the questionnaire you are consenting to your anonymous information being used for research on lifestyle in young people diagnosed with cancer.

Prize draw

Once you have completed the survey you will be given the option to enter a prize draw for Amazon vouchers. There will be three prize draws, 1st draw winning £50, 2nd draw winning £30 and 3rd draw winning £15.

Please take time to read over the information about the study on the following page.

There are no right or wrong answers to any of the questions. Please be as honest as possible when answering. Your answers will be confidential and will not be shared with anyone other than the research team.
