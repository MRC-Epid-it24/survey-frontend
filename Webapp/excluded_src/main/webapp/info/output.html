Welcome to Intake24

Intake24 is an open source self-completed computerised dietary recall system based on multiple-pass 24-hour recall. The online system offers similar data quality to interviewer-led recalls at a significantly lower cost.

Try a demo » Watch a video »

Nutrient output

Data are output in spreadsheet format (.csv) that can be opened with Excel or similar software for easy data manipulation and analysis.

Nutrient intake can be output as soon as the participant has finished entering their data.

Data output includes:

  • Energy (kJ and kcal)
  • Macronutrients – Fat, Carbohydrate, Protein and Alcohol, Total Sugars, Non-Milk Extrinsic Sugars (NMES), Saturated fat and Cholesterol
  • Micronutrients – Vitamin A, Vitamin D, Vitamin C, Vitamin E, Folate, Sodium, Calcium, Iron, Zinc and Selenium
  • Food Groups
